Return and exchange policy

At PicolaStore , we strive to ensure your complete satisfaction with every purchase. If, for any reason, you are not entirely pleased
with your order, we are here to help.

14-Day Return Window: You have 14 days from the date of purchase to return eligible items for a refund or exchange. Eligibility Criteria:

  • Items must be unused and in the same condition as received.
  • Original tags and packaging must be intact.
  • Personalized or customized items are not eligible for return.

    • How to Initiate a Return

    1. Contact our customer service team at picolastore1@gmail.com to initiate the return process.
    2. State your order number and reason for the return
    3. Our team will guide you on the next steps. ​

    Refund Processing

    Upon receiving your returned item, we will inspect it and notify you of the status. If approved, your refund will be processed, and
    a credit will automatically be
    applied to your original payment method.

    Exchanges:

    If you would like to exchange an item, please follow the return process and place a new order for the desired item.
    Shipping Costs: Shipping costs for returns are the customer's responsibility unless the return is due to a mistake on our part. ​

    Questions or Concerns

    If you have any questions or concerns regarding our return policy, please feel free to contact us.​
